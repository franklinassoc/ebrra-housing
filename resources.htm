Process Overview

EBR RA Housing Rehab, Construction and Inspections

  1. EBR RA staff access the "New Request Form" page of this website to enter each new case; click "Submit" .
  2. A confirmation email is sent to staff person submitting the request and to Franklin Associates, alerting them to the request.
  3. System creates a case folder within Google Drive. 
  4. System generates a summary report of the request data and sends it to Google Drive. It is placed in the corresponding case folder.
  5. EBRRA staff access this Google Drive case folder and upload supporting documents for each property, as required.
  6. Franklin staff screen requests for completeness; requesting supplemental information as required.
  7. When complete, Franklin staff forward a notification to The Workforce Group. Case is queued for initial inspection scheduling.
  8. Inspection findings are summarized in a pair of reports: one itemizes the costs, the second lists the scope only. 
  9. The Workforce Group delivers these to Franklin Associates by uploading to Google Drive where they are reviewed.
  10. Accepted inspection reports are moved to the corresponding project case folder and a notice is sent to EBR Redevelopment Authority.
  11.  A decision is made whether the case qualifies for renovation. ($25K cap)
  12. If "yes", case enters the renovation administration process below. If "no", applicant is notified of decision by EBR Redevelopment Authority and case files are archived.
